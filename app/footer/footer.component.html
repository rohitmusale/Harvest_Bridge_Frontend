Harvest Bridge

The need for a farmer's portal arises from the challenges faced by farmers in accessing critical information, services, and markets that can help them enhance their agricultural practices and productivity. Many farmers lack access to reliable sources of information and resources, making it difficult for them to make informed decisions about their crops, soil management, and marketing strategies.
